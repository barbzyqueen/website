About Us

Welcome to Minds & Souls

Minds & Souls Foundation is an organisation
dedicated to helping needy children in the
African continent. The aim is to ensure that the
less privileged are given equal opportunities by
providing them access to basic needs.


The Minds and Souls Foundation is hereby
providing a donation platform, in addition to
humanitarian support from the general public, to
assist in providing the basic schooling amenities
to educate an African Child, and making him
become a leader in the future that is not too
distant.
